Skillet Lasagna

Description

This no-bake skillet lasagna is made right on your stovetop and is a fast and easy alternative to store-bought hamburger mixes!

Ingredients

  • 0.5 pound ground beef
  • 0.5 jar (28 ounce) spagetti sauce
  • 1 can dicet tomatoes
  • half onion, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil (Optional)
  • 2 teaspooons dried oregano (Optional)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 cups dried malfada noodles
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
